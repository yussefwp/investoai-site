Frequently Asked Questions

What is InvestoAI?

InvestoAI is an AI-powered stock analysis app that provides instant technical analysis, support and resistance levels, confidence scores, and AI-written insights for any U.S. stock. Our analysis engine was built by investors with over 30 years of experience.

How do I analyze a stock?

Simply open the app, type a stock ticker (like AAPL, TSLA, or NVDA) in the search bar on the Home screen or the Analyze tab, and tap Analyze. You'll receive a full technical breakdown within seconds.

Is InvestoAI financial advice?

No. InvestoAI provides technical analysis for informational and educational purposes only. It is not financial advice, and you should always do your own research and consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

What's included in the Free plan?

Free users can perform up to 3 stock analyses per day, view breaking market news, and explore the app's features.

What do I get with Basic ($9.99/month)?

Unlimited stock analyses, full AI-powered insights and summaries, personal watchlist with live prices, and complete technical indicator breakdowns.

What do I get with Pro ($24.99/month)?

Everything in Basic, plus daily Top 10 AI stock picks with entry prices, target levels, stop losses, and AI-generated reasoning for each pick. Our scanner analyzes the entire U.S. market twice daily.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find InvestoAI, and you can change or cancel your plan there. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

Open InvestoAI, go to the Account tab, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to make the original purchase.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at support@investoai.net with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and we will process your request within 30 days. Please cancel any active subscriptions through your Apple ID settings before requesting deletion.

What data sources does InvestoAI use?

We use institutional-grade market data providers for real-time and historical stock prices, volume data, and financial news. Our AI analysis engine processes this data using proven technical strategies developed by experienced investors.
